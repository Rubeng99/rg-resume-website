ECE 5 Project Website

During my last quarter at UCSD I decided to take a lower division Electrical Engineering course titled "ECE 5". I took this course because it did not feel right to be in the Jacob's School of Engineering without haven taken an ECE course. In this course we learned how to use Onshape to design and print our 3D chassis. We also learned the skills of soldering and working with hardware components. I took the initiative of leading a team of 4 people by organizing meet up times to work on the build and fix any hardware issues that occur.
